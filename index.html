Washington's marijuana retail stores

Dots show licensed marijuana retail stores as of July 7, 2014.

These 24 licensed stores can open at 8 a.m. on Tuesday, July 8, though some are planning to open later this week.

For more on legal pot in Washington, see the Seattle Times' story on the first retail licenses and this Q&A on the hows and whys of legal marijuana.
